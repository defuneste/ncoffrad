Brown Mountain offers 34 miles of trails for ATV, Trail bikes and Four-wheel drive! The trails cover all the levels of difficulty from easy to very challenging!

Trails Overview:

Check the difficulty and if your vehicle is authorized

Trail bikers are free to use all the trails but there are more restrictions on drivers of ATV and Four-wheel drive vehicles (4WD).

Green represents the easiest trails, yellow tends to provide more challenges and black are the hardest ones.

Enjoy!
